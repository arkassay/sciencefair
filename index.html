Welcome to the DigitasLBi NY Science Fair 2014 Kickoff!

1. Create your team:

Teams consist of 2-3 people (at least 1 developer and 1 UX)

How to sign up:
Here at the end of this meeting, or email your teams to Amanda Kassay, Min Chung, and/or Itai Asseo

2. Submit your project idea:

In your submission, answer the following questions:

  • In general, what is the technology you have chosen and how will you use it for your project?
  • How is this technology or process used today?
  • How do you envision that this technology or process will be used in the future?
  • Why should we as Marketers care about this technology or process?
  • What is the level of effort needed to bring this technology to market? What type of skills are required?

Please include with your submission each team members name, title and email addresses.

3. Present your project:

Along with your working project, create a tri-fold poster board. This should include:

  • Information from your initial submission.
  • Images/diagrams
  • Description of the development process
